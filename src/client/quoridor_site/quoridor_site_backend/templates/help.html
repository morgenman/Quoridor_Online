How to Play

The Goal of the Game

  • The goal of the game in Quoridor is to reach the opposite wall of the player's starting position.
  • Each turn, you as the player have the choice of either moving your piece or placing a wall
  • You must make a move each turn, once you run out of walls, you must move your pawn

    • Moves

  • Players can move one square at a time, horizonally or vertically, forwards or backwards.
  • Players cannot move diagonally
  • Players cannot move through a wall, they must move around it

    • Walls

  • In a two player game each player is given 10 walls, in a 4 player game each player gets 5 walls
  • Walls can be placed either horizontally or vertically
  • A path to the goal must be left open for every player, you will not be able to place a wall the blocks a player in.

    • Jumping over players

  • Everytime two players face each other on neighboring squares, the player who is moving will have the option to jump over the other player
  • The player can jump over the other pawn in a straight line
  • If and only if the linear path behind the other player is blocked by a wall, you can jump diagonally to either side of the opposing player
